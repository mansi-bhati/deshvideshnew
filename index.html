Our Story
"Travel is not just movement — it is transformation. Every journey should leave you a little more alive."

Desh Videsh Tours & Travels was born from a lifetime of leadership and a deep love for the world's wonders. Mr. Sanjeev Bhati, having shaped thousands of young lives as the founder of Sahara Education, turned his attention to a new canvas — the open road, the open sea, and the open sky.

His vision was simple: to bring the same standard of care, trust, and excellence he built in education into the world of travel. Every itinerary we craft carries that legacy — meticulous, personal, and deeply human.
