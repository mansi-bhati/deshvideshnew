Day-by-Day

Your itinerary

A thoughtfully paced journey through Sri Lanka's most iconic landscapes and cultural wonders.

1
Day 1
Arrival in Colombo — Begin the Journey
Your Desh Videsh representative meets you at Bandaranaike International Airport with a warm welcome. Transfer to your hotel in Colombo for a comfortable check-in. Evening at leisure — stroll along the Galle Face promenade and soak in the ocean breeze. Dinner at hotel.
2
Day 2
Colombo City Tour + Transfer to Kandy
After breakfast, explore Colombo's most iconic landmarks before heading to the Cultural Capital — Kandy.
  • Colombo Lotus Tower — Sri Lanka's tallest tower
  • Gangarama Temple — a serene spiritual experience
  • Scenic drive to Kandy through lush highlands
  • Check-in to Kandy hotel · Dinner included
3
Day 3
Kandy — Elephants, Temples & Gardens
A full day immersed in Kandy's breathtaking nature and rich culture.
  • Pinnawala Elephant Orphanage — watch giants roam free
  • Peradeniya Royal Botanical Garden
  • Gem Museum Visit
  • Temple of the Sacred Tooth Relic (Sri Dalada Maligawa)
  • Traditional Cultural Show in the evening
4
Day 4
Kandy Upper Lake + Transfer to Bentota
After breakfast, enjoy a picturesque drive around Kandy's Upper Lake before your journey south to the golden coast of Bentota — Sri Lanka's premier beach resort destination.
  • Kandy Upper Lake Drive — misty & magnificent
  • Afternoon transfer to Bentota
  • Check-in to beachfront hotel · Dinner included
5
Day 5
Bentota — Safari, Spices & the Sea
An action-packed day along Sri Lanka's glittering southwestern coast.
  • Bentota Spice Garden & Turtle Hatchery
  • Thrilling River Boat Safari on Bentota River
  • Ambalangoda Mask Painting Workshop
  • Free evening on the beach
6
Day 6
Bentota — Water Sports & Leisure
Your day to unwind and thrill in equal measure. The turquoise waters of Bentota are yours to explore.
  • Water Sports: Jet Ski, Banana Boat, Snorkelling
  • Leisurely beach afternoon
  • Sunset dinner by the ocean
7
Day 7
Departure — Until Next Time, Sri Lanka
After a leisurely breakfast, transfer to Colombo Airport for your return flight. Carry home nothing but golden memories — Sri Lanka has a way of staying with you long after you've left.
